Welcome to Torus Chess!

This is a unique variant of chess played on a torus (donut shape) with a reflection. This allows for topological weirdness while still being a playable game.

  • Horizontal Wrapping: Moving off the right edge brings you to the left edge
  • Vertical Reflection: Moving off the top edge brings you to the bottom edge, but reflected

Good luck!
